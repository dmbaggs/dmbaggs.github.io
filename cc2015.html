Cuba Creatives 2015

25 University of Oregon students, faculty and staff visited Cuba in spring 2015 to document Cuba’s arts, culture and people at a historic moment in time. The group profiled six of Cuba's creatives - people who embody the spirit of their country’s past and the possibility of its future.

Year: 2015

Client: University of Oregon School of Journalism and Communication

Objective: create a website and iPad experience showcasing the profiles and trip.
